Biodiversity & the Parks

Throughout the world, industrial development, habitat loss, and changing climate patterns due to human activities threaten the existence of much of the planet’s biodiversity. In the United States, the National Park System has provided a case study in the successful management of critical habitats for the protection of endangered and threatened species… understand exactly which parks have more endangered species...
